Statement of the Problem

1.) How does the geometric structure of the Marikina River (such as curvature, width variation, and narrow sections) affect the movement, escape, and accumulation of plastic debris during flood conditions?

2.) Can a geometry-driven mathematical model accurately predict locations where plastic debris is most likely to accumulate or escape during flooding events?

3.) How can the predicted accumulation zones be used to improve the placement of trash traps and flood mitigation strategies along the Marikina River?
