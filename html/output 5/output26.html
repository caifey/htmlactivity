Statement of the Problem

1.) How do Acer mono and A. pseudosieboldianum differ in their photosynthetic parameters (Amax, LSP, LCP) under varying light intensities?

2.) What are the effects of different light intensities on the growth performance of the two maple species?

3.) How do the physiological indicators (MDA levels, antioxidant enzyme activities, proline and soluble protein content) of seedlings change under light stress?

4.) What morphological adjustments (RSR, SLA) do seedlings develop to adapt to reduced light conditions?

5.) Which maple species demonstrates stronger adaptability and tolerance to light variation?
