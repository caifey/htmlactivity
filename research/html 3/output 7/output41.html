Recommendations

To improve the quality of the product, future researchers should explore advanced pulping and refining techniques to enhance the paper’s texture and ink receptivity. It is also recommended to conduct a Life Cycle Analysis (LCA) to formally compare the water and energy savings of grass paper against traditional wood-pulp methods. Additionally, studies focusing on the economic scalability of Bermuda Grass paper are necessary to ensure it can be produced at a price point competitive with commercial brands. Lastly, the use of various natural binders should be tested to increase the paper’s tensile strength for more demanding applications.
