Recommendations

The present study reveals that Saluyot (C.Olitorius) extract contains alkaloids and tannins. Saluyot (C. Olitorius) was also found to be effective against Trichophyton mentagrophytes with the Minimum Inhibitory Concentration of 25%. A significant difference exists between the effectiveness of Saluyot (C. Olitorius) and the commercially-available antifungal (Canestin).
