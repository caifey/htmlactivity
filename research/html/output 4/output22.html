Results

Table 1 shows that alkaloids and tannins are present in the extract of Saluyot. The presence of alkaloids increases the potential of Saluyot as antifungal.

Table 2 shows that the Control (Canesten) displayed the widest ZOI of 22 mm. The pure extract (T1) showed the second widest ZOI of 14.67 mm. The 25% Saluyot extract (T4) is considered as the Minimum Inhibitory Concentration since it still inhibited the growth of Trichophyton mentagrophytes.
