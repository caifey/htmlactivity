Results

The study revealed that 45% of respondents found Bermuda Grass paper to be durable, while 65% considered it a viable sustainable alternative to commercial paper. Although 80% of participants recognized its positive environmental impact, 45% emphasized the need for further improvements in texture and quality. The data suggests that while the product is promising, its success depends on its accessibility and affordability. Experimental trials confirmed that Bermuda Grass is the most effective raw material, provided the fibers undergo thorough mechanical pounding and blending
