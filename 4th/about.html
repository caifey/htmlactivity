About CoffeeBara

At CoffeeBara, we are very proud to give students and families a quiet place to relax. Our brand is all about peace and calm, which you can feel in every warm latte and sweet pastry we make. Inspired by the chill nature of the capybara, CoffeeBara is a cozy spot built for anyone who needs to take a break. We are on a mission to serve great coffee and delicious treats in a friendly space where everyone feels welcome. Come join us for a drink and enjoy the calm vibes at CoffeeBara.
